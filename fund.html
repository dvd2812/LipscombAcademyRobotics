How to donate online:

  • Go to www.lipscomb.edu/give
  • Under "Give a Gift," click "get started"
  • Under "Select an Area," choose "Lipscomb Academy"
  • Under "Select a designation," leave it blank
  • Choose your type of gift, size, and if it is in honor or memory of anyone
  • Under "Special Instructions," type "Lipscomb Academy Robotics Team"
  • Click "Add this gift"
  • Continue website instructions to checkout
